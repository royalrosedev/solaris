The Problems

Climate change

As the world's population grows, so does the demand for energy. The current energy infrastructure is not sustainable and is contributing to climate change. The world needs reliable, clean, and renewable energy.

Electricity inequality

Communities in underdeveloped or remote locations often grapple with expensive and unreliable electricity, and rely on potentially expensive or pollutive power generation.

Solar is imperfect

Solar can and has been used to help with these problems, but it is an imperfect solution. We want to target and solve multiple deficiencies that solar faces to make these problems better.
