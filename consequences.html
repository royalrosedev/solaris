We are committed to...

Keeping our skies clear.

Space pollution is a real problem. We are committed to keeping our skies clear and our satellites safe. With robost protocols for satellite retirement, we will ensure near-Earth space is safe to travel within.

Minimizing disruption.

We are committed to minimizing the impact of this light from our satellites on local ecosystems. We work with scientists to understand the impact of our technology and to mitigate it.

Protecting our home.

Solaris focuses vast quanitites of light, which can be dangerous if not properly managed. We have mechanical and software systems in place to ensure that our mirrors cannot focus intense levels of light on Earth.
